---++ Installation Instructions
There are no special steps required. Install and enable ExportExcelPlugin and its dependencies - that's it.
In order to make a regular HTML table exportable, extend its class definitions by <code>exportable</code>, eg.

<verbatim>
<table class="myclass exportable">...</table>
</verbatim>
